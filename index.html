What is Tempo?

Tempo is a tool to help you measure your site's overall speed and weight, giving you a quick read on how the site is functioning, with the ability to dig into the numbers as you see fit.

$ cd your_repo_root/repo_name
$ git fetch origin
$ git checkout gh-pages

How does Tempo Work?

Tempo measures the speed and weight of a site by TKTK, utlizing Highcharts tools to show a handy graph of just how heavy and slow a page may be (hopefully you're getting more medals of honor than deathly slow skulls).
